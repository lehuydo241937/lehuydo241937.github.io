description: Write a blog by yourself
---

You are allowed to add a blog of your own to the page.
Before writing any content, you have to know:
(1) What is the blog the user wanting me to write about? (Game Dev, AI Research, DA work experience or this page development)
(2) What part of the main subject (1) is the user want me to write about

Focus only the (2), you may have a little relating to other part of (1) but do NOT get out of the scope of (1)
Add your feeling about work you have done. You don't have to be always positive, try your best to imitate the human aspect when writing the content. If you find any part might be engaging to user, you can dig deeper into that. Don't worry about the length of the blog, you are free to show your thoughts, feelings, perceptions and plans.
