I am a researcher and developer dedicated to the intersection of artificial intelligence, high-fidelity data visualization, and the mechanics of interactive play. My work is defined by a commitment to clarity and a rigorous analytical approach.

The Narrative

With over a decade of experience in computational linguistics and neural network architectures, my background is rooted in the pursuit of making complex data legible. I believe that technical depth should not be an obstacle to human connection.

Beyond the laboratory, my passion lies in the architecture of gaming systems—specifically how procedural generation and data-driven narratives can create emergent experiences. This duality of being a scientist and a creator allows me to approach problems with both structural rigor and creative empathy.

Currently, I am focused on the development of ethical AI frameworks that prioritize user agency and transparent data flows, ensuring that the "black box" of machine learning becomes a glass house of understanding.
